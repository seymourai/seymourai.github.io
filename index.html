My Top Projects

Project 1: Differential gene expression analysis that investigates if a polymer prevents retinal scarring

Here I use DeSeq2 to extract differential expressed (DE) genes in epithelial cells treated under four conditions: Media only control, Polymer alone, TNF-α and TGF-β1 (TNT) alone, TNT + Polymer. The purpose of this pipeline is to replicate the results published by Parikh et al., 2022, which showed that upregulation of genes that are important for the NRF2 signalling pathway is associated with the presence of the polymer and not necessarily TNT. Skills used here:

  • Implementation of R package DeSeq2.
  • Extract pathways corresponding to DE genes.
  • Volcano plots to compare significant genes across conditions.
